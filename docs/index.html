How to Use

Tested only with Buy and Sell-type groups such as The University Trade and The Katipunan Trade with DESKTOP Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. Works either logged-in or out.

Only top-level comments are considered; comments in comments will not be considered. This script is provided as-is with no warranty and may stop working at any time.

  1. Drag this link Get Random Bumper to your bookmarks bar
  2. Go to your post's permalink by clicking on the date below the poster's name (link should look like https://www.facebook.com/groups/TheUniversityTrade/posts/1572485526454048/).
  3. Open your browser console to see progress (optional)
  4. Click on the Get Random Bumper bookmark you added in Step 1.
  5. Wait until it completes.
